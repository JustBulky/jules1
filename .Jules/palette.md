## 2024-05-23 - Palette's Philosophy

**Learning:** UX is about small touches.
**Action:** Always verify with accessibility tools.

## 2024-05-23 - Accessibility for Icon-Only Buttons

**Learning:** Icon-only buttons (like Send or Close) are invisible to screen readers without labels.
**Action:** Always add `aria-label` to icon-only buttons, and use `title` for tooltip-like behavior on desktop.
